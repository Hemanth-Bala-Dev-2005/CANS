About DES

DES is a symmetric-key block cipher with 64-bit block size and 56-bit key. It uses 16 rounds of Feistel network with expansion, substitution (S-boxes), and permutation (P-box) operations. The algorithm was once the standard but is now considered insecure due to its small key size.

DES: 16 hex characters (64 bits, 8 bytes) | Uses 56 bits + 8 parity bits
